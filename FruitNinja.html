Fruit Ninja
Halfbrick Fridays
Halfbrick Studios, an Australia-based video game company, came up with the idea for Fruit Ninja via a traditional meeting that occured around five times a year. These meetings essentially served as a group brainstorm session to come up with new, exciting game ideas. Many of Halfbrick's other games, such as Age of Zombies and Jetpack Joyride, were bred from these meetings. Teamwork really does make the dream work!
Think Fast!
If you ever want to put your reaction time and patience to the test, this will always be the game for you! With a blend of fresh, juicy fruits, colorful blades, and choice between two different gamemodes, you can live up to the name of "Fruit Ninja!" In Classic Mode, you'll be able to cut plenty off fruit, but don't let them fall to the ground, and don't cut the bombs that appear! In Arcade Mode, it's you against the clock, so don't stop cutting!
