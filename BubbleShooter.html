Puzzle Bobble?
In 1994, the videogame company Taito Corporation in Tokyo, Japan created a game called "Puzzle Bobble," launching in Western countries in the same year. The game would go on to define a genre of video games that companies such as Absolutist Ltd continued to refine and develop in the Western world. This company is also responsible for coining the name "Bubble Shooter!"
Pop em' All!
You point, you shoot, you pop, repeat. What more do you need? Following Absolutist Ltd's footsteps, other game companies included narratives, power-ups, and other add-ons that added more depth to the game. But at heart, Bubble Shooters are all about the saturated colors, shiny bubbles, and satisfying sound design! Watch someone play the original game here!
