Power Couple!
Very few prolific video games seem to possess small developer teams, but an exception is Temple Run's Imangi Studios. Run by Natalia Luckyanova and her husband, Keith Shepherd, the two found a common interest in the iPhone upon its release. They were both working as Software Developers at the time, but quit their jobs to pursue their passion of creating video games! Of course, such a passion warrants serious hard work and many failures, but these two pushed through and created the perfect smartphone game!

What Kind of Animal...
Your name is Guy Dangerous, an Australian archaeologist and adventurer looking for an ancient artifact somewhere deep in a temple. Lucky for you, you find it at the foot of the entrance unattended. But it was a trap! Out from the shadows comes an... ape? a Wendigo? Anyways, doesn't matter because it's angry and hungry; two things that don't mix well together. You turn around and start running, and there happens to be coins along the way! Atleast you won't go home empty handed, eh? Well, that is if you manage to keep running, because aside from that giant beast on your tail, there is an unpredictable landscape ahead. Onwards to riches!
